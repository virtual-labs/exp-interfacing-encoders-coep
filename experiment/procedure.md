### Procedure

Step 1 - Select Input Pin For Incremental Encoder.<br>
Step 2 - Click on "GENERATE CODE" button to generate code.<br>
Step 3 - Click on "COMPILE" button to compile code.<br>
Step 4 - Click on "EXECUTE" button to execute code.<br>
Step 5 - Click on "DOWNLOAD" button to download code.<br>
Step 6 - Click on green circle to connect pin.<br>
![*image* ](images/connection.PNG)<br>
Step 7 - Click on any green rectangle to connect pin.<br>
![*image* ](images/connection1.PNG)<br>
Step 8 - Click on plus Button to increse Speed and click on minus button to decrease speed<br>
![*image* ](images/connection2.PNG)<br>
Step 9 -Observe Oscilloscope to generated graph waves<br>
![*image* ](images/connection3.PNG)<br>
Step 10 - Click on "Next level" button<br>

If you want to download Result click on "DOWNLOAD REPORT" button.
