### Procedure

Step 1 - Select Input Pin For Incremental Encoder.
Step 2 - Click on "GENERATE CODE" button to generate code.
Step 3 - Click on "COMPILE" button to compile code.
Step 4 - Click on "EXECUTE" button to execute code.
Step 4 - Click on "DOWNLOAD" button to download code.
Step 5 - Click on green circle to connect pin.
Step 6 - Select and Change Potentiometer Configuration then see the graph waves.

If you want to download code click on "DOWNLOAD" button.
