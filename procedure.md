### Procedure

Step 1 - Select Input Pin For Incremental Encoder.<br>
Step 2 - Click on "GENERATE CODE" button to generate code.<br>
Step 3 - Click on "COMPILE" button to compile code.<br>
Step 4 - Click on "EXECUTE" button to execute code.<br>
Step 5 - Click on "DOWNLOAD" button to download code.<br>
Step 6 - Select input pin for Incremental encoder<br>
![*image* ](images/conf1.PNG)<br>
Step 7 - Click on green dot to connect pin.<br>
![*image* ](images/connection.PNG)<br>
Step 8 - Click on any green rectangle to connect pin.<br>
![*image* ](images/rectangle.PNG)<br>
Step 9 - Click on plus Button to increse Speed and click on minus button to decrease speed<br>
![*image* ](images/speed.PNG)<br>
